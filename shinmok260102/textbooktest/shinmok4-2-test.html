Example 2 · The Debate · Conclusion

▸ Example 2 — Making Music with AI

AI programs may lead to changes not only in the art world but also in the music industry. These programs are already able to compose songs, and they give you many options. For example, you can choose the style and key, such as C major or A minor. You can also set how long the song will be. Once you've entered the information, the program will produce a song that you can edit or use as it is. Furthermore, computer-generated imagery (commonly known as CGI) has opened up new possibilities in the music industry. Virtual influencers with extremely realistic faces, bodies, and voices have already released several songs. Some of them have many followers on social media, just like human singers. These types of virtual musicians are able to speak and move realistically with the help of their human creators.

▸ The Debate on Using AI

Many AI programs are open to the public, which means they can be used freely and conveniently. However, these programs have sparked a debate over their impact on humans. Since AI has the potential to learn skills and perform them better than humans, some people view it as a threat. They worry that AI will replace humans in more and more areas. There are also concerns that AI may imitate the styles of human artists without their consent. On the other hand, others think that AI cannot exceed human imagination and that it is still dependent on human assistance. This is because the data that AI uses can have flaws or limitations. A dependence on imperfect data with little diversity eventually limits the output of AI programs. Human imagination, on the other hand, has no limits or boundaries.

▸ Conclusion

What kind of future do you think lies ahead of us? Will more people use AI programs to boost their creativity and productivity? Or will people grow tired of the limitations of AI tools? AI provides many benefits and new opportunities. What we need to do is figure out how to use AI tools ethically and think about how humans and AI can collaborate.

3. 다음 글의 (A)·(B)·(C)에서 어법에 맞는 표현으로 가장 적절한 것은?
Since AI has the potential to learn skills and perform them (A) good / better than humans, some people view it as a threat. They worry that AI will replace humans in (B) more and more / more or more areas. On the other hand, AI's data can have flaws and (C) little / few diversity, eventually limiting its output.
①(A)good (B)more and more (C)little ②(A)better (B)more and more (C)little ③(A)better (B)more or more (C)few ④(A)good (B)more and more (C)few ⑤(A)better (B)more and more (C)few
정답 & 해설
정답: ② better / more and more / little
📌 (A) better — 뒤에 than humans → 비교급 필요. good의 비교급 = better (불규칙: good-better-best)

📌 (B) more and more — 비교급 + and + 비교급 = "점점 더 ~한"
▸ more and more (점점 더 많은) / bigger and bigger / better and better
※ more or more는 비문

📌 (C) little — diversity(다양성)는 불가산명사 → little 사용
본문 표현: "with little diversity"(다양성이 거의 없는)

📌 핵심 — 가산/불가산 + few/little
가산명사 (셀 수 있음):
• few(거의 없는, 부정) / a few(약간, 긍정) / many(많은)
예: few books, a few students

불가산명사 (셀 수 없음):
• little(거의 없는, 부정) / a little(약간, 긍정) / much(많은)
예: little water, much information

diversity, information, advice, music, water, knowledge 등은 모두 불가산.

4. 다음 밑줄 친 부분 중 어법상 적절하지 않은 것은?

①②③④⑤
정답 & 해설
정답: ② which → that
📌 5개 밑줄 분석

① which — 계속적 용법 관계대명사. 앞 문장 전체를 받음 ✓

② which ✗ 정답
구조: concerns ___ AI may imitate the styles
명사 concerns(우려)와 뒤 절(완전한 절)이 동격 관계 ("AI가 모방할 거라는 우려").
▸ 동격 접속사는 that이지 which 아님 → that으로 교체

③ that — think의 목적절을 이끄는 명사절 접속사 ✓
④ that — 선행사 the data, 뒤 절 목적어 비어있음 → 목적격 관계대명사 ✓
⑤ which — 계속적 용법, 선행사 Human imagination ✓

📌 핵심 — 동격 that vs 관계대명사 which/that
동격 that (명사 + that + 완전한 절):
• the fact that AI can compose
• concerns that AI may imitate
• the news that he won
▸ 완전한 절 + that = 동격
▸ which 사용 불가

관계대명사 which/that (명사 + which/that + 불완전 절):
• the data which/that AI uses ___ (목적어 비어있음)

📌 함정 식별
명사 + 빈칸 + 완전한 절 → 동격 that
명사 + 빈칸 + 불완전 절 → 관계대명사 which/that

5. 빈칸 (A)·(B)에 들어갈 말로 가장 적절한 것은?
(A) __ entirely on imperfect data, AI programs eventually face limits in their output. Human imagination, (B) __ has no such boundaries, easily surpasses these constraints.
①(A)Depend (B)which ②(A)Depending (B)which ③(A)Depended (B)what ④(A)Depending (B)what ⑤(A)Depend (B)that
정답 & 해설
정답: ② Depending / which
📌 (A) Depending — 분사구문
주어 AI programs가 의존하는 주체 → 현재분사(-ing)
▸ Depending entirely on imperfect data = "불완전한 데이터에 전적으로 의존하면서"

※ Depend(원형)는 부사구 시작 자리에 부적합
※ Depended(과거분사)는 의존 받는 객체일 때 사용 — 여기선 부적합

📌 (B) which — 계속적 용법
구조: "Human imagination, ___ has no such boundaries, easily surpasses these constraints"
▸ 본 절: Human imagination ... easily surpasses these constraints (주어 + 본 동사)
▸ 콤마 사이 삽입: which has no such boundaries — 계속적 용법 관계대명사 절
▸ 선행사 Human imagination(사물) → which
※ what은 선행사 포함 (선행사 못 가짐) ✗
※ that은 계속적 용법(콤마 사이) 사용 불가 ✗

📌 핵심 정리
분사구문 (콤마 + 본 절):
• Depending on X, Y... = "X에 의존하면서 Y하다" (능동)
• Based on X, Y... = "X에 기반해 Y하다" (수동)
• Compared to X, Y... = "X와 비교해 Y하다" (수동)

관계대명사 계속적 용법:
• that은 계속적 용법 사용 불가 ★
• which/who/whose만 사용 가능
• 선행사 사물 → which, 사람 → who

▸ (A) 세 번째 — "On the other hand, others think..."

② (A)-(C)-(B) — 같음 ✗
③ (B)-(A)-(C) — 반대 입장이 위협론보다 먼저 ✗
④ (B)-(C)-(A) ✓ 정답 (논란 발생 → 한 입장 → 반대 입장)
⑤ (C)-(B)-(A) — 위협론이 논란 등장 전에 옴 ✗

• However = 역접 → (B)는 긍정 사실 직후
• Since = 이유 → (C)는 위협론의 근거
• On the other hand = 대조 → (A)는 한 입장 다음의 반대 입장

📌 영어 토론 글 패턴
일반 사실 → 논란 도입 (However) → 한 입장 (Since/Some people) → 반대 입장 (On the other hand/Others)

The debate over AI involves two main perspectives. Those who fear AI argue that it may eventually (1) humans across many fields. Meanwhile, defenders of AI insist that human imagination has no (2) or boundaries, while AI's output is constrained by imperfect data.
(1)                   　　(2)                   
정답 & 해설
정답: (1) replace · (2) limits
📌 (1) 빈칸 분석 — replace
본문 핵심: "AI will replace humans in more and more areas"

📌 (2) 빈칸 분석 — limits
본문 핵심: "Human imagination... has no limits or boundaries"
문맥: "no ___ or boundaries" — 본문 표현 그대로
→ 정답: limits (한계, 복수)

35. 본문 주제(AI의 사용)에 대한 자신의 의견을 다음 조건에 맞춰 영작하시오.
[ 조건 ]
1. 본문의 두 입장 중 하나(위협론 또는 옹호론)를 선택할 것 (선택 명시)
2. 본문에 없는 새로운 근거 1개 제시할 것
3. 영어 두 문장 이상으로 작성 (총 30단어 이상)
4. 'I think' 또는 'In my opinion'으로 시작할 것
　 　 　
정답 & 해설
정답 예시 (옹호론, 38단어): I think AI is not a real threat because it still depends on human data and creativity. Moreover, AI cannot understand human emotions deeply, which means human artists will always remain essential for meaningful artistic expression.
📌 4개 조건 정리 + 충족 분석
① 입장 선택 + 명시 → "I think AI is not a real threat" (옹호론 선택)
② 본문에 없는 새 근거 → "AI cannot understand human emotions deeply" (감정 이해의 한계 — 본문에 없음)
③ 두 문장 이상 + 30단어 이상 → 두 문장, 38단어 ✓
④ 'I think' 시작 → ✓

📌 다른 정답 예시 (위협론 선택)
"In my opinion, AI does pose a real threat to many jobs. Beyond what the passage mentions, AI can also work 24 hours without rest, which makes it impossible for humans to compete in repetitive tasks." (39단어)
